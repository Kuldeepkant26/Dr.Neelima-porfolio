Meet The Doctor

Meet Professor Neelima Gupta, a distinguished medical professional serving as the Director Professor and Head of the Department of Otorhinolaryngology at the University College of Medical Sciences and GTB Hospital, University of Delhi.

With an impressive academic background including MBBS, MS, DNB and MNAMS, Professor Gupta’s expertise in Otorhinolaryngology is well-established. She has been an integral part of the medical field, contributing significantly to patient care, research, and education.

Professor Gupta’s journey includes pivotal roles such as Senior Resident at GTB Hospital, Delhi, and Faculty at UCMS and GTB Hospital, Delhi, where she currently serves as the Head of the Department of ENT. Her leadership has been instrumental in enhancing the department’s capabilities and fostering a conducive learning environment.

Recognized for her contributions, Professor Gupta was awarded the BOYSCAST Fellowship by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, facilitating advanced research in “Chemosensory Research” at the University of Dresden Medical School, Germany. She utilised the fellowship by conducting advanced research in the area of Smell and Taste.

As a dedicated academician, Professor Gupta has been actively involved in guiding postgraduate students in their thesis work, with emphasis on basic and clinical research. She has utilised intramural grants for the thesis work for her postgraduate students. Her PhD co-supervision for a PhD student in Microbiology demonstrates her multidisciplinary and collaborative approach to healthcare.

Professor Gupta’s areas of interest span Rhinology, Allergy and Immunology, Air and Noise pollution’s effects on health, and Chronic Rhinosinusitis. Her commitment to advancing medical knowledge and providing personalized patient care reflects a career marked by excellence.

Step into the realm of Otorhinolaryngology guided by Professor Neelima Gupta, where experience meets compassion, and every patient receives the highest standard of care in her department.
